Save electricity

Here’s how you can make a difference today: Switch off lights, computers, and appliances when not in use. Set your aircon to 24°C. This is the most efficient setting for comfort and savings. Let the sunlight in during the day and use natural ventilation. Unplug chargers and appliances after using them. Let’s work together to create a better future. #YouHaveThePower to save energy! This is a public service announcement in partnership with the Department of Energy.
